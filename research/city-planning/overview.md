## Tax Incentives

**Class 6b:** Sterling Bay's 4510 W Ann Lurie Place — 147,500 SF speculative industrial. Approved Jul 2023. ~$4.7M tax reduction over 12 years. Partner: Affinius Capital. Loan: First National Bank of Omaha.
