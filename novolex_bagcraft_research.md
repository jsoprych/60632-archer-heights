## 1. NOVOLEX OWNERSHIP STRUCTURE

**Current (2022-present):**
- **Majority owner:** Apollo Global Management (Apollo Funds) -- acquired majority stake in April 2022
- **Minority owner:** Carlyle Group -- retains a strategic minority stake after the 2022 sale
- **Management equity:** Chairman and CEO Stan Bikulege and senior management hold equity
- **Deal value:** ~$6 billion (reported by Bloomberg, Feb 2022)

**Ownership history:**
- 2003-2012: Wind Point Partners (PE) + management -- founded as Hilex Poly via carve-out from Sonoco
- 2012: TPG Growth (minority); Wind Point remained majority
- 2016: Carlyle Group -- ~$2.5B acquisition; doubled company size during ownership
- 2022-present: Apollo (majority) + Carlyle (minority) -- $6B deal; Apollo is controlling PE owner

**Legal entity:** Novolex Holdings LLC (privately held, PE-backed)
**HQ:** Charlotte, NC (3436 Toringdon Way, Suite 100)
**Former names:** Hilex Poly (pre-2014)

## 4. BAGCRAFT PACKAGING

### Acquisition history
- Bagcraft originally BagcraftPapercon, unit of Packaging Dynamics Corporation
- **Novolex acquired Packaging Dynamics on December 8, 2014** from Kohlberg and Company
- $1.9B combined company; Novolex's 3rd acquisition
- Bagcraft operates as 'Bagcraft Packaging, A Novolex Brand'

### 3900 W 43rd St, Chicago, IL 60632
- **Location:** Archer Heights -- right in target 60632 ZIP
- **History:** Founded 1948 as Bagcraft Papercon; ~77 years continuous operation
- **Products:** Paper-based flexible foodservice packaging
- **Estimated ~139 employees** (RocketReach)
- **Revenue attributed to Bagcraft:** ~$250-279M (ZoomInfo / RocketReach)
- **No documented layoffs or WARN notices** at this facility
- One of six Bagcraft manufacturing plants across the US
